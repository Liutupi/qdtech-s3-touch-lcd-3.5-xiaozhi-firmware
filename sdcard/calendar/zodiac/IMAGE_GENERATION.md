# Zodiac image generation record

The twelve source illustrations were generated with OpenAI ImageGen on
2026-07-18 and then mechanically resized to 160x160 RGB JPEG at quality 88 for
the embedded SD-card package.

Shared prompt direction:

> Create a square cute chibi zodiac illustration for a small embedded 3.5-inch
> LCD: [sign-specific mascot]. Use an oversized round head, compact body, big
> gentle sparkling eyes, a friendly expression, deep midnight-blue starry sky,
> simple glowing constellation dots, warm gold plus a sign-specific accent,
> rounded polished children's-storybook and kawaii game-icon styling, an
> uncluttered high-contrast composition readable at 160 pixels, and generous
> padding. No text, letters, numbers, zodiac glyph, border, or watermark. Keep
> a cohesive twelve-image mascot series.

Sign-specific subjects are ram, bull, twins, crab, lion, garden guardian,
balance scale, scorpion, centaur archer, sea-goat, water bearer, and paired
fish. Original generated PNG files remain in the local Codex generated-image
store; the files in `images/` are the device-ready derivatives.
